# Services Procurement (SVCS-PROC): questions waiting for you

## What this domain is
Buy outsourced services under a statement of work (SOW), then track milestones to acceptance and payment.

Procure outsourced services from a firm under a statement of work, not headcount you manage day to day. Author the SOW, take in proposals, and stand up the engagement, then break the work into milestones and deliverables and track each one to acceptance. Settle on what was delivered with milestone-based invoices, handle change orders when scope shifts, and keep a clear record of who accepted what and when. The services firm stays your supplier and the SOW stays your contract; this is where you run the engagement in between.
