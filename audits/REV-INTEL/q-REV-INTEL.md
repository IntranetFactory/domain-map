# Revenue Intelligence (REV-INTEL): questions waiting for you

## What this domain is
See every deal the way your best rep would. Revenue Intelligence reads the calls, emails, meetings, and CRM activity your sales team generates every day and turns them into deal-risk scores, conversation insights, and an accurate roll-up forecast, so managers can see which deals are slipping and where coaching pays off.

---

q1: Sales Engagement publishes conversation insights into Revenue Intelligence, where they feed the "analyze sales trends and patterns" work. Today no one in Revenue Intelligence is named as responsible for that analysis, so the step has no owner. Who should own it?
- a) The Revenue Operations Analyst runs the analysis and is also accountable for it.
- b) The Revenue Operations Analyst runs the analysis and the Sales Manager is accountable.
- c) The Sales Enablement Coach runs the analysis (this persona is the primary user of the conversation module) and the Sales Manager is accountable.
- d) Leave it unassigned for now.

Recommended: b. "Analyze sales trends and patterns" is analytical RevOps work, so the Revenue Operations Analyst is the natural Responsible owner; the Sales Manager owns the outcome of pipeline analysis at most flagship Revenue Intelligence vendors (Gong, Clari, BoostUp all surface trend analysis to the manager for sign-off), which maps cleanly to Accountable.
